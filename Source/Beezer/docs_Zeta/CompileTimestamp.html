 Compile Timestamp

The file settings/_bzr_ctstamp.txt contains the date & time when your copy of Beezer was compiled. It is a generate file and do NOT modify it!

This is often useful in knowing how old your Beezer is, and also useful for debugging purposes (in situations when even the app refuses to start). You can also see this date & time in your About box.

This file is internally used by Beezer and in general you shouldn't tamper with or bother about it.
